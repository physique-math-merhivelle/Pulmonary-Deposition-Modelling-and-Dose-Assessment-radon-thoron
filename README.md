# Pulmonary Deposition & Dose Assessment Model

**Master 2 Thesis** | Nuclear Physics, Dosimetry & Radiation Protection  
University of Yaoundé I, Cameroon

Modeling of pulmonary deposition and dose assessment induced by radon (Rn-222) and thoron (Rn-220) progeny in indoor air. 
Based on ICRP 66/100/103/130/137 respiratory tract models with classification of aerosols into free, attached, and nucleation modes. 

Validated using field measurement data from Mobilong and Fongo-Tongo, Cameroon.
